给定 k,a,n,d,p  
f(i)=1^k+2^k+3^k+......+i^k  
g(x)=f(1)+f(2)+f(3)+....+f(x)  
求(g(a)+g(a+d)+g(a+2d)+......+g(a+nd))mod p

对于所有数据

0<=a,n,d<=123456789  
p==1234567891
